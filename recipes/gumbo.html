Gumbo

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup canola oil
  • 4 cups chopped onion
  • 2 cups chopped celery
  • 2 cups chopped green pepper
  • 1 cup sliced green onions
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 8 cups water
  • 4 cups sliced okra
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 2 tablespoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 6 cups small shrimp, rinsed and drained, or seafood of your choice
  • 1 cup minced fresh parsley
  • tablespoons Cajun seasoning

Directions

  1. In a large, deep skillet over medium-low heat, melt butter, then add flour
  2. Cook, stirring constantly, until dark caramel colored, about 10 minutes.
  3. Add onions, peppers, and celery, and stir until softened, about 5 minutes more
  4. Stir in garlic and sausage, then season with Cajun seasoning, salt, and pepper
  5. Stir in bay leaf, diced tomatoes, and chicken broth and bring to a boil
  6. heat to low and simmer until thickened, stirring occasionally, about 1 hour
  7. In the last 10 minutes of cooking, add shrimp. Once shrimp is pink and cooked through, taste and adjust seasonings
  8. spoon on top of white rice.
